Define

To understand the customer's needs, so as to find possible solutions; while considering the entire solution space.

Activities covered under Define Stage

  • Business Model Hypotheses, Mapping and Team Building
  • Market research and customer needs analysis
  • Solution Concept Development

Key objectives of the above mentioned activities

  • Making sure the team is well organised and skilled enough to develop the business.
  • Being strategic to choose where, how and who is going to be interviewed during the market research and consider different demographics among the target market.
  • Building a good relationship with some of the customers during the market research in order to engage them throughout the process till deployment.
×

Design

a detailed design of the proposed solution and shape the product’s or service’s desirability and feasibility.

Activities covered under Design Stage

  • Competition Analysis, Revenue Model Design & Service Experience Cycle Map
  • Prototype design & Intellectual Property
  • Product Specifications, Tools & Technology

Key objectives of the above mentioned activities

  • Making sure the product is innovative enough to make a difference over the competition and to create compelling values for the customers.
  • Leveraging modern tools and technologies to efficiently design the product or service and engage previously secured customers to test the concept and desirability of the design.
  • Having the product/service Specifications and metrics well analysed and considering Trade-offs where necessary.
×

Develop

The product or service that serves the identified needs ensuring viability and sustainability of the business, as well as it’s impact on the environment.

Activities covered under Develop Stage

  • Product or Service Development Process
  • Product/Service Development economics & Financial Modeling
  • Prototype development, Testing & MVP Packaging

Key objectives of the above mentioned activities

  • Working on financial projections and making sure to determine whether the product/service will make a viable business or if not, a decision will be made to either pivot or change the business idea completely.
  • To never fall in love with your prototype. Instead, putting user experience feedback on the center of decision making will turn it into a better prototype.
  • Engaging already connected/secured customers to have their feedback on the functionality and desirability of the product.
×

Deploy

The minimum viable product (MVP) for customer validation, traction building and go-to market strategy testing.

Activities covered under Deploy Stage

  • MVP Validation Reports
  • Business documents Development
  • Partnerships & Investment Readiness

Key objectives of the above mentioned activities

  • Packaging the product as a Minimum Viable Product and deploying to the early secured customers for feedback especially on the Price, Functionality, and Packaging.
  • Documenting everything and focusing on the story, traction and testimonies from the early customers to have a concrete reason for investment to scale.
  • Looking for strategic Partnerships and mentorship to help scale the business.

About us

From Idea to Business

The 4Ds Metrics model is an approach designed for the rapid and efficient development of desirable and feasible product or service to a viable business model. This is achieved by applying a combination of the iterative 4Ds namely; Define, Design, Develop and Deploy as well as bringing into play design thinking. In simple words, 4Ds Metrics represent a process of developing a new business idea to a viable business.

Our Services

Business Diagnostics & Development
